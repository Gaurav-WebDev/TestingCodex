Modern challenges youth face

Study overload & exam stress

Pressure to perform, fear of failure, poor sleep cycles, and constant deadlines can push your mind into survival mode.

Social media comparison

Scrolling can make life look perfect for everyone else and leave you feeling behind, disconnected, or not enough.

Loneliness & identity pressure

Navigating friendships, family expectations, career confusion, and self-worth can feel overwhelming at any age.

Daily wellbeing toolkit

Choose one tool now. Small actions, repeated daily, can reduce anxiety and improve emotional resilience.

60-second grounding

Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, and 1 you taste. This helps calm racing thoughts.

Breathing reset

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 6 seconds. Repeat for one minute.

    Need urgent help right now?

    If you are in danger, have thoughts of self-harm, or feel unsafe, contact local emergency services immediately.

    • Call your local emergency number now.
    • Reach out to a trusted adult, counselor, or campus wellbeing team.
    • Use your country's 24/7 suicide & crisis lifeline.
